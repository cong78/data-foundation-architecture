## Why a Data Foundation Matters

An enterprise data and AI strategy sets the ambition, but it does not create a dependable supply of trusted data. When every initiative integrates sources, interprets meaning, implements controls, and proves quality independently, delivery slows, costs multiply, and trust becomes inconsistent. AI agents increase the urgency because they can act on data at greater speed and scale.

<div class="foundation-why-chain" aria-label="How a data foundation connects enterprise strategy to outcomes">
  <div><small>Data and AI ambition</small><strong>Improve decisions, products, operations, and intelligent automation.</strong></div>
  <i aria-hidden="true">→</i>
  <div class="foundation-why-chain__focus"><small>Data foundation</small><strong>Provide reusable products, shared meaning, governed access, and observable trust.</strong></div>
  <i aria-hidden="true">→</i>
  <div><small>Outcome at scale</small><strong>People, applications, models, and AI agents use dependable data without rebuilding the foundation.</strong></div>
</div>

The data foundation closes the gap between ambition and execution. It turns distributed source data into owned, contracted, discoverable, governed, and observable data products, then makes those products usable through fit-for-purpose interfaces. This creates one reusable trust layer for analytics, applications, sharing, models, and autonomous agents.

Its strategic value is not another data platform. It is the ability to **reuse data safely, prove that it can be trusted, and scale human and agentic consumption without scaling fragmentation**.

The foundation has one primary delivery story: **Frame → Establish → Deliver → Use → Operate**. Every architecture view, service, standard, template, and reference solution supports one or more stages of this journey.

## How the Data Foundation Works

The foundation is one system described through six connected views. Each view answers a different question and must not be used as a substitute for another.

<div class="foundation-structure-map" role="img" aria-label="Six connected structural views of the data foundation">
  <div class="foundation-structure-rule"><strong>Structural rule</strong><span>Journey directs · architecture places · planes assure · services deliver · products create value · runway scales</span></div>
  <a class="foundation-structure-row" href="#why-each-stage-matters"><span>1</span><div><small>Direct</small><strong>Five journey stages</strong></div><p>Move work from agreed scope through establishment, delivery, use, and reliable operation.</p><em>What happens next?</em></a>
  <a class="foundation-structure-row" href="../../architecture/overview/"><span>2</span><div><small>Place</small><strong>Five architecture layers</strong></div><p>Place experience, governance, data products, cross-cutting services, and platform runtime in the solution.</p><em>Where does it belong?</em></a>
  <a class="foundation-structure-row" href="../../architecture/target-architecture/"><span>3</span><div><small>Assure</small><strong>Six architecture planes</strong></div><p>Test every design across experience, control, data, AI, observability, and security responsibilities.</p><em>Is the design complete?</em></a>
  <a class="foundation-structure-row foundation-structure-row--focus" href="../../services/"><span>4</span><div><small>Deliver</small><strong>Nine foundation services</strong></div><p>Turn architecture into reusable, owned, supported, and measurable capabilities for users and systems.</p><em>Who builds and operates it?</em></a>
  <a class="foundation-structure-row" href="../../architecture/data-foundation-model/#data-alignment-patterns"><span>5</span><div><small>Create value</small><strong>Three data product patterns</strong></div><p>Provide source-aligned, aggregate, and consumer-aligned products with explicit promises and owners.</p><em>What trusted output is delivered?</em></a>
  <a class="foundation-structure-row" href="../../runway/"><span>6</span><div><small>Scale</small><strong>Four runway phases</strong></div><p>Grow adoption from a controlled baseline to reuse, AI readiness, and ecosystem sharing.</p><em>How does capability mature?</em></a>
</div>

Foundation services are the delivery entities in this structure. Each service owns a user outcome, interfaces, controls, SLOs, support, runbooks, evidence, and improvement. Architecture defines what must exist; services make it usable and operable; data products carry the resulting value to consumers.

Every service is agentic by design. The Data Service AI Assistant coordinates service specialist agents, while published contracts and policy bound their work and deterministic services remain responsible for authorization, state change, and evidence.
